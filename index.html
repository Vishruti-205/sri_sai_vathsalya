Empowering Students with Intellectual Challenges

Jnanajyothi is a transformative initiative dedicated to supporting students with intellectual challenges. Our mission is to provide them with a free residential facility, offering specialized educational programs, personalized care plans, and a nurturing environment to foster their overall development.

This newly launched initiative has made a strong start, currently supporting 70 students with comprehensive facilities. From day one, we have been dedicated to ensuring that our students receive the best possible education, resources, and care to help them succeed.

Join us in our mission to make a difference in the lives of these incredible students. Your support and contributions can help us continue to provide the necessary resources and opportunities for their growth and success.

Our Programs

Guided Learning

Students with intellectual challenges engage in learning activities with the support of dedicated teachers who provide the necessary guidance and encouragement.

Play and Exploration

Our students enjoy playful and exploratory activities with a variety of toys, fostering creativity, imagination, and social interaction.

Therapeutic Activities

We offer a range of therapeutic activities designed to support the physical, emotional, and cognitive development of our students.

Life Skills Training

Our life skills training program helps students develop essential skills for daily living, promoting independence and self-confidence.

Outdoor Activities

Our outdoor activities encourage physical fitness and social interaction, providing students with opportunities to explore and enjoy nature.
